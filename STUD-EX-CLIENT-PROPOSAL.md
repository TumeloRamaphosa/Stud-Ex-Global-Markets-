## What Does This Platform Do For You?

### Deal Flow & Investor Network
- **Find and connect** with global investors, entrepreneurs, and business partners
- **Track every deal** from first contact to close with real-time status updates, progress tracking, and team collaboration
- **Secure messaging** with file sharing, so sensitive negotiations stay in one protected space
- **AI-powered deal insights** that predict which deals are likely to close and recommend next actions

### AI Marketing That Runs Itself
This is what sets Stud-Ex apart. Our Larry AI Marketing Engine automates your entire content pipeline:

**Step 1 — Hook Generation:** AI creates proven viral hooks using 7 psychological formulas (Person-Conflict, Budget Transformation, Before/After, and more) tailored to your niche.

**Step 2 — Content Creation:** Automatically generates professional 6-slide carousel posts with the structure that drives engagement: Hook → Problem → Discovery → Transformation → Reinforcement → Call to Action.

**Step 3 — Image & Video Production:** AI generates custom visuals using Gemini 3 Pro image technology and creates videos using Remotion — no designer or video editor needed.

**Step 4 — Multi-Platform Publishing:** One click publishes to TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook, X (Twitter), Threads, Pinterest, Reddit, and Bluesky simultaneously.

**Step 5 — Performance Analytics:** Tracks views, likes, shares, comments, follower growth, and most importantly — actual revenue attribution per post.

**Step 6 — Automatic Optimisation:** The system analyses what's working, creates variations of winning content, and drops what isn't performing. Every day it gets smarter.

### Revenue Intelligence
- **Daily automated reports** delivered by 7:00 AM telling you exactly what to do
- **Two-axis diagnostic framework** that distinguishes between "your hook isn't working" and "your call-to-action needs fixing" — so you know precisely what to change
- **Revenue attribution** that connects social media posts to actual paying customers (via RevenueCat integration)
- **Competitor research** that shows you what's working in your niche before you spend a cent

### Time & Project Management
- **Built-in time tracker** with category logging (Meetings, Analysis, Legal, Administrative)
- **Calendar integration** for events, calls, and networking sessions
- **Project-based hour tracking** for billing and profitability analysis
